Account Verification (KYC) Questions

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process that Perlaplay uses to confirm the identity of its players. This industry-standard procedure helps prevent fraud, money laundering, and underage gambling. At Perlaplay, we take player safety seriously and comply with international gaming regulations to ensure a secure environment for all users.

What documents do I need for account verification at Perlaplay?

To complete your Perlaplay account verification, you'll typically need to provide:

  • A valid government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or official government correspondence)
