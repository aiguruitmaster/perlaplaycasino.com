Account Verification (KYC) Questions

Why do I need to verify my Perlaplay account?

Account verification, also known as Know Your Customer (KYC), is a standard industry practice required by gaming regulations. At Perlaplay, we implement KYC procedures to prevent fraud, protect your account from unauthorized access, ensure responsible gaming practices, and comply with international anti-money laundering regulations. This process helps maintain a safe and secure gaming environment for all our players.

What documents do I need for verification?

To complete your Perlaplay account verification, you'll typically need to provide the following documents:

  • A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or official government correspondence)
  • Proof of payment method (photo of your credit/debit card or screenshot of your e-wallet account)

All documents must be clear, valid, and show your full name matching your Perlaplay account registration details.

How long does the verification process take?

The verification team at Perlaplay typically reviews and approves documents within 24-48 hours. During peak periods, this might extend to 72 hours. We recommend submitting your documents as soon as you register to avoid any delays when requesting your first withdrawal.
