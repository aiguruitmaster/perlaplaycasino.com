Understanding Wagering Requirements

While bonuses at Perlaplay are generous, it's essential to understand the wagering requirements attached to promotional offers. Wagering requirements, also called playthrough or rollover requirements, specify how many times you must wager bonus funds before you can withdraw associated winnings.

How Wagering Requirements Work

When you claim a bonus at Perlaplay, the wagering requirement determines how much you need to bet before converting bonus funds to withdrawable cash. For example, if you receive a $100 bonus with a 30x wagering requirement, you must place $3,000 in total bets before withdrawal is permitted.

Key points about wagering requirements at Perlaplay include:

  • Requirements typically range from 25x to 50x the bonus amount
  • Some promotions require wagering on deposit plus bonus combined
  • Different games contribute varying percentages toward meeting requirements
  • Slots usually contribute 100%, while table games may contribute 10-20%
  • Time limits apply—bonuses must be wagered within a specified period
  • Maximum bet limits may apply while wagering bonus funds

Tips for Meeting Wagering Requirements

To successfully complete wagering requirements at Perlaplay, consider these strategies:

  • Check game weighting before starting to play with bonus funds
  • Monitor your progress through the casino's bonus tracker
  • Stay within maximum bet limits to avoid voiding your bonus
  • Complete wagering before the expiration deadline
